📋 Quick Fixes

  1. Hard Refresh: Press Ctrl + F5 or Ctrl + Shift + R
  2. Clear Browser Cache: Ctrl + Shift + Delete → Select "All time" → Clear
  3. Try Different Browser: Chrome or Edge recommended
  4. Disable Browser Extensions: Ad blockers might interfere
  5. Check Console: Press F12 and look for detailed errors
